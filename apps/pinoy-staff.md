## What It Does

Pinoy Staff is a household staff management app built for Filipino families. Keep detailed profiles for every employee — drivers, nannies, maids, gardeners, cooks — and manage their wages, documents, and schedules in one private, easy-to-use app. All data stays on your device with optional iCloud sync.

## Features

**Staff Profiles**
Create detailed records for each household employee including contact info, role, and employment history.

**Payment Tracking**
Log wages, bonuses, and benefits so you always have a clear record of what's been paid.

**Document Storage**
Store IDs, contracts, and other important documents directly in the app for easy access.

**Work Scheduling**
Assign and manage shifts so everyone knows their schedule.

**Privacy First**
All data is stored locally on your device. Optional iCloud sync keeps your records backed up and available across your Apple devices.

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 16, 2026**

Pinoy Staff does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. All information you enter is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, data is synced privately through your own iCloud account and is not accessible to the developer.

There are no analytics, no advertising, and no accounts required.

## Support

**What iOS version is required?**
iOS 17.6 or later. iPhone only.

**Does the app require an internet connection?**
No. The app works fully offline. An internet connection is only used for optional iCloud sync.

**How does iCloud sync work?**
Your data syncs privately through your own iCloud account. The developer has no access to your data.

**Is my data backed up?**
If iCloud sync is enabled, your data is backed up automatically. Otherwise, use your device's iCloud backup to preserve your records.
