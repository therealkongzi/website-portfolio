## What It Does

10 Min Math is focused, adaptive math practice designed for elementary school kids. No cartoon distractions, no random drills — just smart practice that targets exactly what each child needs to work on. Each session, your child answers math facts on a large, easy-to-tap keypad. The app tracks every answer and automatically serves up the facts that need the most work. When a fact is consistently answered correctly and quickly, it turns green on the mastery grid. The goal: fill the whole grid.

## Features

**Adaptive Practice**
10 Min Math learns as your child practices. It spots weak facts and brings them back more often until they're truly mastered — not just guessed correctly once.

**Mastery Grid**
Every fact is displayed on a visual grid. Facts turn green as they're mastered, giving kids a clear, motivating picture of their progress.

**Built for Families**
Every child gets their own profile with separate progress tracking, mastery grid, and daily streak. Siblings never mix up their data.

**What Your Child Practices**
- Addition — up to 100
- Subtraction — up to 100
- Multiplication — through the 12× table
- Division — through the 12× table

You control which operations are active and the number range in Settings.

**Parent Features**
- Progress dashboard with accuracy chart and streak tracking
- Full mastery grid — see every fact at a glance
- Weekly progress report you can share with a teacher or co-parent
- Parent PIN to lock Settings away from little fingers
- No ads, no in-app distractions

## Support

**What iOS version is required?**
iOS 16.6 or later. iPhone only.

**Can I use the app on multiple devices?**
Yes. Sign in with the same Apple ID and tap Restore Purchases to unlock Premium on any device.

**How do I restore a purchase after reinstalling?**
Open the app, go to Settings, and tap **Restore Purchases**.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → 10 Min Math → Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the current billing period ends.

**Is my child's data backed up?**
Data is stored locally on your device. Use iCloud device backup to preserve progress when switching phones.
