## What It Does

Daily Chengyu brings the rich world of Chinese idioms to your fingertips. Each day you receive a new chengyu — a four-character expression essential for Chinese language fluency — complete with character breakdowns, pinyin pronunciation, English translation, and its fascinating historical origin. Whether you're a language student, a culture enthusiast, or simply curious, Daily Chengyu makes learning one idiom at a time genuinely enjoyable.

## Features

**Daily Idiom**
Receive a new chengyu every day. Revisit yesterday's with a single tap. Beautiful, easy-to-read cards for every idiom.

**In-Depth Explanations**
Each chengyu comes with a character-by-character breakdown, pinyin pronunciation guide, English meaning, and historical origin story.

**Comprehensive Collection**
5 carefully selected chengyus in the free version. Upgrade to Premium for the full collection of 700+, from everyday expressions to literary classics.

**Search**
Find any chengyu instantly using the character-based search.

**Premium**
Unlock 700+ chengyus, save favorites for quick reference, and enjoy an ad-free experience.
