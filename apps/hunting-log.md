## What It Does

Hunting Log is a companion app for **Red Dead Redemption II** that helps you track every animal in the game's compendium. Mark creatures as studied or hunted, browse stunning visuals of each animal, and sort or search your progress however you like — all for free.

Whether you're going for 100% completion or just keeping tabs on what you've taken down, Hunting Log keeps your progress organized and at your fingertips.

## Features

**Animal Tracking**
Mark any animal as studied or hunted with a single tap. Your progress is always saved locally on your device.

**Compendium Visuals**
Browse pictures of every creature for quick reference while you play.

**Smart Search**
Find any animal instantly with the built-in search field.

**Flexible Sorting**
Organize by alphabetical order, compendium order, or location — whatever suits your play style.

**Completely Free**
No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no hidden costs.

**Last updated: June 16, 2026**

## Support

**What iOS version is required?**
iOS 16.6 or later. iPhone only.

**Does the app require an internet connection?**
No. The app works fully offline.

**Is my progress backed up?**
Progress is stored locally. Use iCloud device backup to preserve it when switching phones.
