**Protein-Sparing Modified Fast** — The comprehensive PSMF tracking app designed for medically-supervised weight loss.

> **Medical Disclaimer:** PSMF is a very low-calorie diet that requires medical supervision. This app is a support tool and does not replace professional healthcare guidance. Always consult your doctor before starting PSMF.

## Features

- **Macro Calculator** — Personalized protein, carb, and fat targets using the YMCA body fat formula
- **Daily Food Logging** — Log meals, save favorites, and monitor macros throughout the day
- **Hydration Tracking** — Smart water goals based on body weight with visual progress indicators
- **Wellness Check-ins** — Track mood, energy, and hunger with trend analysis over time
- **Progress Analytics** — Weight loss charts and body composition snapshots
- **Diet Scheduling** — Automated refeed day and diet break management
- **Educational Resources** — Built-in PSMF guidance, safety information, and meal planning tips
- **Full Privacy** — All data stored locally on your device; nothing is ever shared externally

## Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 13, 2026**

PSMF Diet does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All information you enter — including weight, food logs, and wellness check-ins — is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server or third party. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no accounts required.
