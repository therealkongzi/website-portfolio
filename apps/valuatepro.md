## What It Does

ValuatePro helps business owners determine their company's value using the same methodologies used by investment banks and business brokers. Whether you're considering a sale, seeking investment, or simply curious about what your business is worth, ValuatePro gives you a professional estimate — right on your device, with no financial expertise required.

## Features

**Three Valuation Methods**
Choose the approach that fits your business: Revenue Multiple for growing or subscription-based businesses, Profit Multiple for businesses with consistent earnings, or Cash Flow Analysis for a deeper look at what your business generates.

**Professional Reports**
Upgrade to generate PDF-quality reports suitable for investor presentations and business plans.

**Real Market Methodologies**
Built on the same valuation frameworks used by investment banks and business brokers.

**Privacy-Focused**
All calculations happen on your device. No data is collected or sent anywhere.
